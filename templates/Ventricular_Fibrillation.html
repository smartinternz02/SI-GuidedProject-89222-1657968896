Ventricular_Fibrillation(VF)

ECG Findings

  1. Fibrillation waves of varying amplitude and shape.
  2. No identifiable P waves, QRS complexes, or T waves.
  3. Heart rate anywhere between 150 to 500 per minute.

Types of VF on ECG:

Coarse VF:

  Majority of the waveforms measure 3 mm or greater.

Fine VF:

 Majority of the waveforms measure less than 3 mm.
